Traffic jam means a long line of vehicles stuck in jam. 
It is a common scene in big cities and towns.It has created a 
deplorable condition in our regular life. There are many causes of traffic jams. Increasing the number of buses, 
trucks, autorickshaws, rickshaws are the main causes of traffic jams. Overpopulation are also a cause.On the other hand, the traffic control system 
in our country is not developed.Drivers are also responsible for this problem. 
They have a lack of knowledge about traffic rules. Drivers overtaking tendency and parking here and there are one more 
causes of traffic jams. It causes untold suffering to the passengers and the movement of the vehicles. The problem can 
be solved by taking some steps. Traffic rules should be imposed strictly.
